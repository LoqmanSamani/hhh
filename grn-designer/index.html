About GRN-Designer

The GRN-Designer algorithm is an advanced computational framework designed for modeling, optimizing, and engineering genetic regulatory networks (GRNs). It leverages AI-driven techniques to predict regulatory interactions and enhance synthetic biology applications.

How It Works

GRN-Designer utilizes machine learning and systems biology principles to analyze regulatory interactions. Key features include:

  • Network inference from gene expression data
  • Optimization of synthetic GRNs
  • Predictive modeling for genetic circuits

Results

Our approach has been validated across multiple datasets, demonstrating high predictive accuracy and efficiency in designing functional genetic networks.
